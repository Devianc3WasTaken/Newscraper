Coronavirus information: How to stay safe

How do I protect myself?
Wash hands for about 20 seconds with soap and hot water or user a sanitiser gel.
Use a tissue for coughs and sneezes
Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth with unwashed hands.
Avoid close contact with people who are unwell
If you think you might have the virus, Ring 111 if you are in the UK, do NOT go to a GP surgery, pharmacy or hospital
